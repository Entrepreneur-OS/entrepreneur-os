# Entrepreneur-OS

> An AI-native knowledge operating system for becoming an entrepreneur.

A learning-in-public knowledge graph. It turns one engineer's transition into
entrepreneurship into a durable, versioned asset: each business concept is a
**knowledge object** — not a definition, but an explanation of *why the concept
exists, how experts reason with it, and how to wield it with an AI.*

## Why it exists

In the age of AI, fluency in the precise language and mental models of business is
leverage: a sharply-framed prompt unlocks far richer help than a vague one. This
repo builds that fluency one concept at a time, and compounds it over years.

## How it's organized

- `concepts/` — one file per concept (see `templates/concept.md`).
- `docs/` — context, vision, and design decisions.
- `CLAUDE.md` / `AGENTS.md` — the operating contract for AI collaborators.

## The learning loop

GitHub is the source of truth. Open questions are **Issues**, progress lives on the
**Project** board, and every concept lands via a **Pull Request** that preserves the
reasoning. Domains are **labels**; the graph grows by linking concepts together.

## Status

Bootstrapping — deliberately lean. The structure earns its complexity by growing
into it.
